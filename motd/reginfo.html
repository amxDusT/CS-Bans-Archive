Nickname registration

Benefits

  • Protects your nickname, rank, and identity in general from being compromised
  • Eligibility for one-week VIP privileges after playing for 30 hours in our servers
  • Promotional codes

Registering your nickname

  • In-game:
    • While in our servers, type /reg <e-mail> <in-game password>
    • (Example: /reg dust.pro@gmail.com dust123)
    • Note: You will register the nickname you're currently using.
  • In our forum:
    • Register your nickname at https://csamx.net/regnick

Logging-in

  • Open the game's console and submit the following:
    1. name "your registered nickname"
    2. (Example: name "AmX_DusT")
    3. setinfo  _pw "registered password"
    4. (Example: setinfo  _pw "dust123")

Notes

  • It may take up to 24 hours for a nickname to be registered
    • Use the forum registration if your nickname is still not registered after 24 hours
  • Reset your forgotten password at https://csamx.net/forgot
  • If you have problems logging-in, please contact us via:
    • Forum: https://csamx.net/
    • Discord: discord.csamx.net
